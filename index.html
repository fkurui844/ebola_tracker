Key Facts

  • • Caused by Bundibugyo virus — NO approved vaccine or specific antiviral
  • • WHO declared PHEIC on 17 May 2026 (IHR Article 12)
  • • Historical CFR for Bundibugyo: 30–50%; current confirmed CFR ≈ 13.4%
  • • GeneXpert platform CANNOT detect Bundibugyo virus (WHO alert)
  • • Treatment center attacked/burned in Rwampara (21 May) and Mongbwalu (23 May)
  • • Oxford ChAdOx1 vaccine candidate in animal trials; human trials in 2–3 months
  • • US pledged $112M bilateral + $350M via OCHA for response and humanitarian aid
  • • 17th Ebola outbreak in DRC since 1976 — first BDBV outbreak since 2012
